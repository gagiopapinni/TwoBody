Binary System

This is a visualization of a binary star system - the most common among multiple-star systems. Take a look at available features below.

Trajectory feature lets you to visualize orbits of stars

Stick featue keeps mass-center of binary system at the center of screen, so the system itself would loose it's velocity and would not slip away from you

Grid feature helps you to visualize mass and gravitational field of a star. ( This one is gonna be impressive:) )

Click on a star once to enable velocity controls. Press and move to change it's position.
